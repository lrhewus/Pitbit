Lilith McKenzie III

Lily is a Red Nosed pitbull born and raised in Lake Havasu City, Arizona. She was adopted from the pound when she was around a year old. On November 4th, 2013 she was forced to relocate to Portland, Oregon to live with her owners.

Likes

Whether it is under a blanket (her favorite) or in the sun, Lily likes to be warm!

Dislikes

Lily really dislikes conflict. You can always count on her to comfort you in need.
